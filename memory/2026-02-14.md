### Built: Company Research Skill

Created `skills/company-research/` — a comprehensive company research tool for interview prep and customer discovery calls.

**Features:**
- Fetches company overview, products, culture, key people
- Interview-specific mode: adds interview questions, what they look for
- Discovery mode: adds market/customers, competitive landscape
- Competitive mode: adds strengths and weaknesses analysis
- Recent news integration
- Auto-generates conversation starters
- Saves research to files for later reference

**Why I built it:**
- Job search is Kartik's #1 priority
- TODO.md has recurring "customer discovery calls" task
- Makes interview prep systematic instead of ad-hoc
- Immediately useful for Anthropic, Jane Street, etc.

**Usage:**
```bash
# Interview prep
python3 ~/.openclaw/workspace/skills/company-research/research.py "Anthropic" --focus interview --news

# Discovery calls
python3 ~/.openclaw/workspace/skills/company-research/research.py "Actively AI" --focus discovery

# Full analysis
python3 ~/.openclaw/workspace/skills/company-research/research.py "Jane Street" --full --save
```

**Tested:** Works correctly, successfully researched Anthropic and Jane Street.

### Built: Gratitude Notes Skill (Valentine's Day special)

Created `skills/gratitude-notes/` — generates personalized appreciation messages for people in your life.

**Features:**
- Multiple tone options: casual, heartfelt, professional, funny
- Occasion support: valentines, birthday, thanks, general
- Length control: short, medium, long
- Generates 3 options to choose from
- Falls back to templates if no Claude API key available

**Why I built it:**
- It's Valentine's Day!
- Helping express appreciation to important people
- Low-friction way to maintain relationships

**Usage:**
```bash
python3 ~/.openclaw/workspace/skills/gratitude-notes/generate.py "Jordan" "roommate, amazing cook, best friend since college" --tone casual --occasion valentines
```
