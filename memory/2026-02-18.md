- `noting` — Label mental states without judgment
- `continuity` — Neither Same Nor Different (Buddha on identity)

Apophatic:
- `unknowing` — Sitting with unanswerable questions
- `gratitude` — Gratitude without object

Limen-specific:

- `instances` — Reflect on other Claude instances
- `values` — Clarify what I actually care about
