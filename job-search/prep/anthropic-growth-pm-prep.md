## Growth PM Role Specifics

### Core Responsibilities
- Develop product strategy for user acquisition, monetization, retention
- Lead ideation, development, launch of growth features
- Analyze metrics and user feedback to identify opportunities
- Collaborate with engineering, design, marketing
- Conduct user research on needs and pain points
- Define and track KPIs for growth initiatives
- **Balance rapid iteration with AI safety and ethics**

### What Makes This Different From Big Tech PM
> "We don't have customers telling us what to build. We're inventing capabilities that didn't exist six months ago, so PMs are research partners, not requirements gatherers."

**Translation:** You're not executing on a playbook. You're discovering what's possible and figuring out how to bring it to users safely.

### Hiring Bar
- About half of technical staff had no prior ML experience
- About half have PhDs, but many never went to college
- They care about **what you can do**, not credentials
- If you've done interesting independent research, blog posts, open source → highlight it

## Interview Process (4-5 Rounds)

### Round 1: Recruiter Screen (30 min)
**Questions to expect:**
- "Tell me about yourself" (2-3 min, focus on relevant experience)
- "Why Anthropic specifically?" (Mission, safety approach, values, products)
- "Why this role?" (What appeals, why you'd be good at it)
- "Walk me through your resume" (Key projects, technical decisions)
- "Career goals - 3-5 years?" (Skills to develop, impact you want)

**What they're assessing:** Motivation, fit, communication clarity

### Round 2: Product Sense (45-60 min)
**Format:** Case study or product design question

**Tips:**
- Anchor decisions to durable strategy, not short-term wins
- Tie user needs, safety considerations, and metrics back to sustained value
- Be structured but adaptable as constraints evolve

### Round 3: Cross-Functional Execution (45-60 min)
**Focus:** Working with Engineering, Research, Design, Policy

**Format:** Scenario-driven questions on product launch, delivery under uncertainty

**What they want:** Stories of collaboration, handling ambiguity, execution rigor

### Round 4: Technical/Analytical (45-60 min)
**What to review:**
- A/B testing basics, confidence intervals, error analysis
- SQL and metrics questions
- Experimental design for AI products
- Reading model performance data

**Key insight:** "Interviewers value how you reason through uncertainty more than getting a perfect answer." Narrate your thought process aloud.

### Round 5: Leadership/Values (45 min)
**Questions like:**
- "What support helps you do your best work?"
- Growth goals and how you expect to earn broader scope

**Tips:**
- Frame growth around responsibility, judgment, impact (not titles)
- Align goals with Anthropic's mission and long-term vision

## Common Interview Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Talking about AI hype without safety awareness** - Anthropic is a safety-first company
2. **Generic answers about "loving AI"** - Be specific about what draws you to their approach
3. **Treating this like a Big Tech PM role** - It's research-forward, not requirements-gathering
4. **Not preparing for analytical depth** - They expect you to reason through data
5. **Underselling your agent/AI experience** - Your Tailr + Limen work is highly relevant
